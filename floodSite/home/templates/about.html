About us


What do we do?

Before the Flood is a motivational website which can help individuals achieve their personal goals and tasks. It's a tool to increase users’ efficiency and productivity. It's a place where people can collaborate, share ideas, encourage and ask questions


What do we have?

Our site consists of these main features:

Forum where users can share and post their ideas and questions
Motivational dashboard with customisable profile and background
Chat room for users to communicate individually or as a group
To-do list so users can see their upcoming goals and tasks
Calender to keep track of all goals and tasks
Motivational tree which grows dynamically whenever users complete tasks, earning them experience points
